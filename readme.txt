This Plugin creates a call to action banner for mobile devices, with settings to change the design, link and media width.

== Description ==

### Beautifully banner to change automatically the phone number 

This plugin change automatically the phone number of your mobile banner.
Only by clicking on the settings page you can enter your phone number with special characters you want. 
An advantage: The banner is fixed on your website and easy to handle!

#### Click on the phone number to start calling

Enter in the settings page your phone number without any special characters and people can call you directly.
It's so easy and practical for visitors!

#### Design? You change it!

* You choose which background color the banner has.
* Define the color of your phone number.
* All color representations, from a word to hexadecimal.

#### When should it appear on my website?

When should it appear? Define the media sizes of your banner for mobile devices. 
Enter your wish width in pixel for more control about your Plugin.

== Installation ==

1. Log in to /wp-admin.
1. Visit Plugins -> Add New
1. Search for 'AWEOS Mobile CTA'
1. Download the plugin.
1. Activate the plugin.
1. Look at your new mobile banner!
